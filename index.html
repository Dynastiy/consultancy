Launch & Grow is a program for entrepreneurs actively pursuing their own startup.

Throughout the program, the focus is to accomplish the following:

Encourage and teach people how to start and manage a business.

Help the business jump start the process of setting up by providing resources and support to help prepare them now and in the future.

Build a community of entrepreneurs who can encourage, teach and support one another.

Evaluation

To successfully complete the program, you must attend 2 out of 4 sessions and submit at least 2 group discussion updates. Startups that complete the course will receive a public Launch & Grow page to certify your completion and graduation from the course.
