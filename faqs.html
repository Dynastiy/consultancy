FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is there a cost to the program?

The registration fee for Launch & Grow is ₦ 50,000. Every other services and resources provided to support founders and their businesses are FREE.

Is there a limit to the number of founders who can participate in the program?

We do not have a limit on the number of participants.

How many hours should I expect to put into this?

For founders in Launch & Grow there is a minimum commitment of 3 hours a week: 2 hours of lecture and one group session.

If I’m doing a business, why should I bother with this? What do I get out of it?

If you’re doing a business, this program will help provide insight on running your company through exposure to best practices, helpful resources, and actionable advice. In addition you get a Free Startup Kit that gives you access to Business Name Registration with CAC, Logo & Branding, Website, Business Plan, Financial Account Setup and Social Media Setup

What are the requirements to complete and pass the program? Are there exams and homework?

There are no exams. The only requirements to pass the program is to submit your weekly updates and attend group sessions.

If I’m working with other people, who should participate?

We recommend inviting your co-founders to participate with you. Designate one founder as your representative for group sessions.

Who is teaching the lectures?

The lectures will be taught by expert consultant and industry leaders with strong background in entrepreneurship.

Where will the lectures be held?

The lectures will be held at Launch & Grow office, at CITEC Villa, Gwarimpa, Abuua.

What happens at the end of the program?

Upon successful completion, your online Launch & Grow will profile will be marked as having graduated the program.
Our team immediately works with you to deliver your Free Startup Kit which contains Business Name Registration with CAC, Logo & Branding, Website, Business Plan, Financial Account Setup and Social Media Setup
In addition, we invite you to seasonal pitch sessions with investors. Also, we connect you with lending partners for loan opportunities.
